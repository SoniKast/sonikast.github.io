Kastology
Kastology is my video series where I look into obscure things and other stuff I like, either to try and give a new perspective, or to bring more awareness to it. I've gone through a variety of topics, such as video games, TV shows, TV networks as a whole and even music. I'm currently working on two huge videos about the band Rhapsody and its guitarist. Stay tuned for that.
