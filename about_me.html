About Me
Hey. I'm SoniKast, more commonly known as Kast. I also go by other names such as Kaz and TheGoku7729.
I'm a 18 year old aspiring game dev from Europe, currently working on a few projects... Mostly trying to make music right now. My most important projects include the Rocket Engine, Sonic Quest, Light and the Keys of Order and Kastology.
In terms of interests I think you already figured out that I really like the Sonic franchise, but I also like Mario, Half-Life, Metroid and Yakuza.
I don't really watch much anime or read much manga but I love One Piece, Dragon Ball, Jojo's Bizarre Adventure and Yu-Gi-Oh.
I LOVE music, and my favorite bands are Crush 40 (above all else), Rhapsody, Luca Turilli (Rhapsody ex-guitarist)'s solo projects such as his first two solo albums and Dreamquest, BOWL (a little obscure Japanese band), Weezer, Green Day, Dionysus, Franz Ferdinand, and I'll probably update this list when I can think of more.
